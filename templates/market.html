Festive & Seasonal Trends

Focus Now (Sep-Oct): Dasara & Diwali

Top sellers:

  • Home Decor: Hand-painted diyas, torans, wall hangings.
  • Gifting: Jewelry, decorative boxes, artisan foods.
  • Apparel: Handloom sarees, kurtas, dupattas in festive colors.

Upcoming (Nov-Jan): Winter & Weddings

Focus on heavier textiles like Pashmina. Wedding season increases demand for luxury sarees and high-end handcrafted gifts.

Digital Growth Tip

Show Your Process

Short videos (Reels/Shorts) of how products are made gain far more engagement than just photos.

Actionable Steps:

  • Create "making-of" videos: Show your hands, tools, or quick time-lapses.
  • Tell a story: Explain the craft history or inspiration in captions.
  • Use relevant hashtags: E.g., #KalamkariArt #HandmadeInHyderabad #IndianArtisan
